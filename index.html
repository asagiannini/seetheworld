About This Site

How we think about the world affects how we experience the world. For many with anxiety or depression, the view of the world is distorted by negative assumption. The following is a guide to identifying the ways in which you think and how you can challenge these ideas.

This is not a stand-in for real therapy, and it is very important that you seek any medical help you may need. It is, however, a simple aide in getting started understanding and reshaping how you think. The following are tools to identify what negative thought patterns you might be vulnerable to.
